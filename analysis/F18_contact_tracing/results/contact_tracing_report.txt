KEY FINDINGS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. BLE can detect contacts at 2m threshold with reasonable accuracy
2. Detection probability decreases with distance as expected
3. May have false positives at borderline distances (2-3m)
4. Temporal confirmation (duration) critical for reducing false positives

RECOMMENDATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Use conservative RSSI threshold (-65 to -70 dBm)
• Require sustained contact (5+ minutes) to reduce false positives
• Combine with movement detection to filter transient encounters
• Account for body orientation effects (LoS vs nLoS)
• Use probabilistic scoring rather than binary detection

LIMITATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
• Distance estimation has ±2m uncertainty (from B5)
• Body shadowing creates 10 dB variability (from A4)
• Multipath interference affects reliability (from A3)
• Static measurements - real walking creates additional challenges
